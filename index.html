About us

At Thai-tzei, noodles are more than just food—they’re a celebration of flavor and culture. Inspired by the vibrant street food of Thailand, we specialize in serving authentic noodle dishes that bring the heart of Thai cuisine to your table.

Every plate is made with care, using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, to ensure a dining experience that’s as delightful as it is delicious. Whether you’re a fan of classic Pad Thai or adventurous Khao Soi, at Thai-tzei, there’s a noodle dish for every taste and mood.

Menu

Explore our menu and discover a world of authentic Thai noodle dishes crafted with love and fresh ingredients. From spicy stir-fries to comforting noodle soups, there's something to satisfy every craving at Thai-tzei!

Starters
  1. Crispy Spring Rolls: Lightly fried rolls filled with vegetables, served with sweet chili sauce.
  2. Tom Yum Soup: A tangy and spicy soup with shrimp, mushrooms, and Thai herbs.
Stir-Fried Noodles
  1. Pad Thai: Stir-fried rice noodles with tamarind sauce, shrimp or chicken, tofu, and crushed peanuts.
  2. Pad See Ew: Wide rice noodles stir-fried with soy sauce, Chinese broccoli, and your choice of protein.
  3. Pad Kee Mao (Drunken Noodles): Spicy stir-fried noodles with Thai basil, chilies, and vegetables.
Soup
Noodles
  1. Kuay Teow Nam: Classic Thai noodle soup with your choice of chicken, beef, or tofu.
  2. Boat Noodles: Rich and flavorful beef or pork noodle soup with aromatic spices.
  3. Tom Yum Noodles: A noodle twist on the iconic spicy and sour soup, with shrimp and herbs.
Curry 
Noodles
  1. Khao Soi: Creamy coconut curry with egg noodles, chicken or beef, and crispy noodle topping.
  2. Kanom Jeen Nam Ya: Fermented rice noodles served with fish-based curry sauce and fresh vegetables.
Drinks
  1. Thai Iced Tea: Sweet, creamy, and refreshing.
  2. Lemongrass Lemonade: A zesty and herbal thirst-quencher.
